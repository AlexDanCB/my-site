My Rules

Base-lines

  1. 1. What is important is the usefulness, after that, “think pretty”.
  2. 2. Correct information equal to redesign almost everything
  3. 3. Rather than imagine what your user needs, ask
  4. 4. Work hard
  5. 5. Synchronised your work-methods with the rest of the team and have a good time
  6. 6. It’s pure luck that you get to be part of a good team ‘🎲’.

Into the project

  1. 7. Get rid of everything that does not work for users and business
  2. 8. Put the same passion when you create that when you destroy
  3. 9. Love your designs like your “summer love”
  4. 10 The falling rule (-not failing- because you are not really failing, you are just falling a little) will be applied always

Into decisions

  1. 11. Risks only worth it if the full team are ok with them
  2. 12. There is no use of jumping into the abyss if someone on the team is not convinced to jump
  3. 13. If you don´t believe in what you created as a team, you won´t be able to defend it
  4. 14. The real victory is not receiving everyone’s applause but create a project where everyone on the team contributed and where everyone was convinced to follow through on those decisions
